Bistec Ranchero

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs thin-sliced beef steak
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1–2 jalapeños (optional), sliced
  • 1/2 cup beef broth or water
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Oil for sautéing

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet and lightly brown the beef on both sides.

2. Add sliced onions, garlic, and jalapeños; sauté until fragrant.

3. Stir in chopped tomatoes and let them soften into a sauce.

4. Add diced potatoes, cumin, salt, and pepper.

5. Pour in broth, cover, and simmer for 20–25 minutes until potatoes are tender.

6. Serve warm with rice or tortillas.

Mexican Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups liquid (broth or water)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1–2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/4 cup finely diced onion
  • 1–2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the rice until lightly golden.

2. Add the diced onion and garlic; cook until softened.

3. Stir in tomato sauce, cumin, bouillon, and salt.

4. Pour in broth or water and bring to a light boil.

5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 18–20 minutes until tender.

6. Fluff with a fork and serve warm.

Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small tomato, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup onion, finely diced
  • 1–2 serrano or jalapeño peppers, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 lime (juice)
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: 1 garlic clove, minced

Instructions

1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

2. Mash gently with a fork, leaving some small chunks for texture.

3. Add diced tomato, onion, peppers, cilantro, and optional garlic.

4. Squeeze lime juice over the mixture and stir gently.

5. Add salt to taste and adjust lime or cilantro as desired.

6. Serve immediately with chips or as a topping for your favorite dishes.
